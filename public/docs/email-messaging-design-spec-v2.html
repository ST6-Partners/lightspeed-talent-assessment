Candidate Email, Handled Inside the App

Every candidate gets a conversation thread that recruiters work right inside the hiring app — sending and receiving real email through SendGrid — while Greenhouse remains the official system of record.

In-app inbox per candidate Recruiters never leave the app Greenhouse stays the record

How candidate email works today

Today, the conversation with a candidate lives in Greenhouse and in recruiters' personal inboxes.

In practice that means a candidate's messages are split across Greenhouse and whichever recruiter happened to email them. There's no single place in the hiring app to see the full back-and-forth with a candidate.

How it will work

The conversation moves into the hiring app. Recruiters read and reply there, the app sends and catches the email, and Greenhouse still gets every message for the record.

✉️

Sends & receives

The app emails candidates through SendGrid and catches their replies — no recruiter inbox in the middle.

💬

One thread each

Every candidate has a single conversation in the app, so the whole history is in one place.

📋

Greenhouse stays the record

Each message is copied into Greenhouse automatically, so the official record stays complete.

The in-app inbox

A recruiter works the conversation inside the app — a small send-and-receive inbox per candidate, separate from their personal email. The candidate just uses normal email and never sees the app.

How a reply finds the right candidate

Each candidate gets a unique reply address, so an incoming reply files itself to the right person automatically.

⚠️

One address note. lightspeedta@st6partners.com works as the reply mailbox. For full automated receiving, the app needs its own subdomain (e.g. reply.st6partners.com) with a mail-routing record — pointing st6partners.com itself at the app would disrupt company email.

Keeping Greenhouse complete

When a candidate moves into the app, their existing email trail comes with them — and from then on, every message is mirrored back to Greenhouse.

What it takes to run

Sending
  • A SendGrid key in the app's settings
  • A from-address and reply-to address (lightspeedta@st6partners.com)
Receiving
  • A dedicated subdomain (e.g. reply.st6partners.com) with a mail-routing record
  • Greenhouse API access for the import and sync (already available)
